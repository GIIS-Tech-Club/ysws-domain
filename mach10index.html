About Us

MACH10 exudes dedication and enthusiasm as a team competing in the F1 in Schools competition. Our group shares an unwavering passion for engineering, innovation, and motorsport. With a clear goal to design, build, and race small-scale Formula 1 cars using advanced tools and technology, we work in close collaboration to create the fastest and most efficient cars, while managing marketing, sponsorships, and presentations.

At MACH10, we firmly believe that teamwork, creativity, and meticulous attention to detail are the cornerstone of success. Each member of our team plays a crucial role, contributing fresh ideas and unique skills, whether in design, aerodynamics testing, or financial management. Our journey within F1 in Schools extends beyond mere competition; it represents our growth as students and future engineers. We continually challenge ourselves to think innovatively, solve problems, and work cohesively. Through our experiences, MACH10 aims to inspire others to explore the world of STEM and uncover the excitement of engineering and innovation.
